Elite tier — deep dive

The full power of
Algosark, unleashed.

Elite isn't just more features — it's a fundamentally different relationship with Algosark. You get a team behind you, not just a platform in front of you.

Pillar 1

Priority API Integration Support

Connecting your brokerage API is the critical step between paper trading and live execution. Elite members skip the queue — our engineering team handles your broker integration personally.

  • Dedicated onboarding engineer assigned within 24 hours
  • Supported: IBKR, Alpaca, IG Group, Saxo, Binance & more
  • Custom API connector built for non-standard brokerages
  • 4-hour response SLA for integration issues
  • Execution quality monitoring & slippage reporting
Pillar 2

Quant Development Team Consultation

Speak directly to the quant engineers and ML researchers who built your strategy. Ask questions, request adjustments, and collaborate on optimisations that no automated system can provide.

  • Monthly 60-minute 1-on-1 strategy review call
  • Direct Slack/email access to your assigned quant
  • Custom parameter tuning: risk thresholds, position sizing, regime filters
  • On-request backtesting on updated datasets
  • Strategy performance attribution report (quarterly)
Pillar 3

Early Access to New Features

Elite members are the first to use every new capability we ship — often weeks before general availability. You shape the product roadmap and get a competitive edge before the market catches up.

  • Beta access to new ML models and strategy types
  • First access to new asset class coverage
  • Private roadmap briefings with the product team
  • Vote on feature prioritisation as an Elite advisor
  • Exclusive Elite community: Discord with live quant Q&A
